Games

MÖBEL

Others trash, Your treasure!

Möbel is a furniture refurbishing game where you get to let your imagination run wild and upcycle the furnishings of your dreams. Keep them for yourself or style and sell them to be able to acquire new and fun pieces to play around with!

FUNGUY

Mushrooms are your business!

Funguy is a cozy resource gathering and adventure game where you play as a businessman that can’t take their life in the corporate hamster wheel anymore and flees the city to go live peacefully in a cabin out in the forest. But things aren't as they seem…

A match made in-game!

In the late summer of 2022 two game creator students decided that they enjoyed making games together so much that Isopoda came to life. We both bonded over enjoying quirky and interesting games that made us feel things and we both strive to create experiences much like that for our players. Isopoda Games is a passion project of ours and is something we spend our free time working on together in our home office in Gothenburg, Sweden .
