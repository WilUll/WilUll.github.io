About Us

In the late summer of 2022 two game creator students decided that they enjoyed making games together so much that Isopoda Games came to life.

We both bonded over enjoying quirky and interesting games that made us feel things and we both strive to create experiences much like that for our players.
Isopoda Games is a passion project of ours and is something we spend our free time working on together in our home office in Gothenburg, Sweden.
